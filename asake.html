ASAKE

About Artist

Asake is a popular Nigerian singer and songwriter, known for his unique blend of Afrobeat, hip-hop, and street pop music. Born in Lagos, Nigeria, his full name is Ahmed Ololade Asake, and he rose to fame with his breakout hit “Lady” in 2020. Asake’s music is characterized by catchy beats, energetic rhythms, and his distinct vocal style, which blends Yoruba and English lyrics. Asake’s sound draws heavily from his Nigerian roots, but it also incorporates influences from global genres, creating a fusion that resonates with audiences worldwide.

His music often addresses themes of love, party life, and personal experiences, making him a relatable figure for many fans. His debut EP, Mr. Money with the Vibe, was released in 2022 and quickly became a commercial success, solidifying his place in the Nigerian music scene.

Asake’s ability to infuse traditional sounds with modern vibes has earned him both critical acclaim and a growing international following. Known for his vibrant performances and charismatic personality, Asake continues to make waves in the music industry, becoming one of the most exciting new talents to emerge from Africa in recent years.
